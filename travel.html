Travel & Overseas Opportunities

Your pathway to working and living abroad

Visa & Travel Services

We handle all aspects of your international travel and work permit applications. Our experienced team ensures smooth processing and proper documentation for your overseas journey.

  • Visa Application: Complete guidance through visa processes
  • Work Permits: Assistance with work authorization documents
  • Travel Planning: Accommodation and relocation support
  • Pre-departure: Orientation and practical guidance

Visa & Permit Types We Assist With

Work Visa

For professionals seeking employment opportunities abroad. We assist with employer sponsorship and work authorization.

Processing Time: 4-8 weeks

Validity: 1-5 years (varies by country)

Cost Range: $200 - $500

Countries:
Germany UK Canada USA
Student Visa

For students enrolled in accredited educational institutions. Full support with documentation and applications.

Processing Time: 3-6 weeks

Validity: Duration of study program

Cost Range: $150 - $400

Countries:
Canada Australia Netherlands Germany
Skilled Migration Visa

For skilled professionals seeking permanent residency. Assessment and application support for skilled migration programs.

Processing Time: 6-12 months

Validity: Permanent or extended temporary

Cost Range: $1,000 - $3,000

Countries:
Australia Canada New Zealand Singapore
Internship/Exchange Visa

For interns and exchange program participants. Support with program placement and visa documentation.

Processing Time: 2-4 weeks

Validity: 3-12 months

Cost Range: $100 - $300

Countries:
USA Germany Netherlands France
